Top Rated Trips

Wild Rapids Expedition

Brace yourself for a wild ride down untamed rapids! This adrenaline-pumping expedition is perfect for thrill-seekers craving excitement. Navigate through challenging Class IV and V rapids, surrounded by breathtaking scenery and adrenaline-fueled action. Experienced guides will lead you through exhilarating drops and heart-racing twists, ensuring an unforgettable adventure for adrenaline junkies.

Family-Friendly Float

Looking for fun for the whole family? Our family-friendly float offers the perfect blend of excitement and safety for adventurers of all ages. Drift down scenic stretches of river, encountering gentle rapids suitable for beginners and young thrill-seekers. Marvel at the beauty of the surrounding landscape while experienced guides ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone. It's the ultimate bonding experience for families looking to create lasting memories together.

Sunset Serenity Tour

Experience the magic of rafting at sunset on our serene evening tour. As the day winds down, embark on a tranquil journey down the river, basking in the golden hues of the setting sun. Glide through calm waters, surrounded by the peace and tranquility of nature. Perfect for couples seeking a romantic adventure or anyone looking to unwind and connect with the beauty of the outdoors. Let the rhythm of the river soothe your soul on this enchanting twilight voyage.
